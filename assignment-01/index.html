Top 7 Healing Songs That Help Me Reset

  1. "Novacane" by Frank Ocean

    This track has a freeing, floaty vibe that helps me mentally drift and reset. It’s like stepping outside the noise and finding stillness in motion.

  2. "Someone You Loved" by Lewis Capaldi

    The emotional depth of this song helps me process my feelings and find solace.

  3. "Non-Stop" by the cast of Hamilton

    This song fuels my drive — it makes me want to work, write, and push forward with the same relentless energy as Alexander Hamilton. It’s my productivity anthem.

  4. "As Long As I Have You" by Dove Cameron

    Originally from Liv and Maddie, but this song stands on its own. It’s so emotional — it captures that ride-or-die feeling when your love for someone exceeds any worldly limitations. It reminds me that no matter what, love can be an anchor and a light.

  5. "Space Between" by Dove Cameron & Sofia Carson

    This duet captures the ache and beauty of growing apart while still holding love. It speaks to the strength of connection even when distance or change enters the picture — a reminder that love can stretch across any space.
